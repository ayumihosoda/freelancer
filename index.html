About Me

Hi! I am Ayumi and I love coding, coffee, floral prints and babies. I am a freelance web developer originally from Shizuoka in Japan and currently living in the SF Bay area. If you are a business needing a new website or a refresh of your site, let's chat!

When I am not working on websites, I am usually busy doing all kinds of fun stuff with my one year old son, Clyde.
